## Content Strategy

Document Structure Matters

```html
<head>
  <title>Meaningful Page Titles - They Matters a lot</title>
</head>
<body>
  <header role="banner">
    <h2 class="site-title">Text Replacement </h2>
  </header>
  <main role="main">
    <article>
      <header>
        <h1>Title</h1> <!-- Page Title -->
      </header>
      <p> ..... </p>
    </article>
  </main>
  <footer role="contentinfo">
    <p>
      Site &copy; 2014
    </p>
  </footer>
</body>
```
